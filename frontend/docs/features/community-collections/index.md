# Community Collections

For a general definition of what a Collection is, see [Collection](/more/glossary/index.md#collection) in the Glossary.

## Overview

Collections in LFX Insights come in two flavors. **Curated Collections** are maintained exclusively by The Linux Foundation and showcase hand-picked groups of projects around themes, foundations, or initiatives. **Community Collections** are created and shared by any registered user, letting anyone in the open source community organize projects around a topic, technology, or use case that matters to them.

## Discovering Collections

The [Discover Collections](https://insights.linuxfoundation.org/collection) page is the starting point for the Collections feature. It presents all collection types on a single page so you can explore what's available without navigating away.

From there you can navigate to three dedicated sections:

- [Curated Collections](https://insights.linuxfoundation.org/collection/curated): full listing of all Linux Foundation–curated collections, sorted by Featured collections by default.
- [Community Collections](https://insights.linuxfoundation.org/collection/community): full listing of all public community-created collections.
- [My Collections](https://insights.linuxfoundation.org/collection/my-collections): your personal hub, requires login.

### Community Collections

The Community Collections list shows all public user-generated collections. For each collection, you are able to access the linked projects, but also to Share, Duplicate and Like the collection. If you like a collection, you will be able to see all your liked collections within your My Collections page.

### My Collections

My Collections is your personal hub for the collections you've created and the ones you've liked. It is split into two subsections:

- **My Created Collections** — a grid of your own collections. You will be able to make changes to the collection at any time.
- **Liked Collections** — collections you've liked, separated by a labelled divider. Can include Curated, Community, or other users' collections.

## How to manage your own Collections

You must be signed in to be able to manage Collections.

### Create a Collection
1. Click **Create collection** from any Collections page (top-right button on the Discover page or any list page)
3. Fill in the collection name, description, visibility setting (Public or Private), and search for and add projects.
4. Save — the new collection appears in your **My Collections** page

### Add a Project to a Collection

An **Add to collection** button is available on every project detail page in LFX Insights. Clicking it opens a modal where you choose which of your existing collections to add the project to. You can also remove pre-selected items before confirming.

### Like a Collection

The heart icon is visible on every collection card and on the Collection Detail page header.
Liked collections appear in the **Liked Collections** subsection of My Collections

### Share a Collection

Click the **Share** button on the Collection Detail page to open the share flow. Available on both Community collections and your own collections.

### Duplicate a Collection

Click the duplicate icon on a collection card or on the Collection Detail page to create a copy under your My Collections.

## Collection Visibility

User-created collections support two visibility states, private and public.
Private collections will only be visible to the user that created them. Public collections will be visibly within Community Collections.
